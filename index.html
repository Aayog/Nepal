Nepal : An Introduction

Nepal, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal (Sanghiya Loktantrik Ganatantra Nepal), is a landlocked central Himalayan country in South Asia. Nepal is divided into 7 states and 77 districts and 753 local units including 6 metropolises, 11 sub-metropolises, 276 municipal councils, 460 villages and 6743 total number of wards. It has a population of 26.4 million and is the 93rd largest country by area.
