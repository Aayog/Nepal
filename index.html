Nepal : An Introduction

Nepal, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal ( Sanghiya Loktantrik Ganatantra Nepal), is a landlocked central Himalayan country in South Asia. Nepal is divided into 7 states and 77 districts and 744 local units including 4 metropolises, 13 sub-metropolises, 246 municipal councils and 481 villages. It has a population of 26.4 million and is the 93rd largest country by area.
